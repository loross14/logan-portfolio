Science · cancer as decoupling

A tumor is a body losing its coupling.

Measure how tightly a tumor's gene expression still tracks the healthy tissue it came from. Call that agreement κ. Across 223 matched TCGA cancers, the tumors with the lowest κ were the ones most likely to kill — the coupling letting go before the patient did.
